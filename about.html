Our Story

How We Started

Built from a simple idea: real estate in Sudurpaschim deserves the same trust and transparency as anywhere else in Nepal.

Sudurpaschim Ghar Jagga karobar began in Dhangadhi with a clear goal — to make buying, selling, and renting property in Kailali district simpler and more transparent. For years, property deals in this region relied heavily on word of mouth, scattered Facebook posts, and middlemen who added cost and confusion to every transaction.

We set out to change that by building a single, organised platform where every listing is verified, every price is clear, and every buyer can speak directly with the seller. Today, we continue to serve families, investors, and businesses across Dhangadhi and the far-western region with the same commitment we started with — honest information, fast communication, and properties worth your time.
